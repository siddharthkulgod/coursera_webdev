Starter Pokemon

Charmander

Charizard is a fire type pokemon. It has 3 evolution stages and it's final evolution is Charizard. It is one of the starters in the Kanto region. When it rains, steam spouts from the end of it's tail!

Squirtle

Squirtle is a water type pokemon. It has 3 evolution stages and it's final evolution is Blastoise. It is one of the starters in the Kanto region. It can squirt out water with vigorous force and retract completely into it's shell!

Bulbasaur

Bulbasaur is a grass type pokemon. It has 3 evolution stages and it's final evolution is Venasaur. It is one of the starters in the Kanto region. There is a plant seed on it's back that slowly grows larger!
